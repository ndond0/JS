The Basic Program

The Basics Program is a series of nine (9) sessions that are scheduled three times per week over a 3 week period. Each session will last approximately one hour each. Here you will learn the fundamental movements of Crossfit. Each day we will focus on selected movements and spend a majority of the hour dissecting each movement. We will conclude every session with a workout, so please be ready to break a sweat!

It is important that you attend every session since each class will build off the last. If for any reason you happen to miss a basics class you will have the option to make up the day(s) with a coach on the side however, this will be an additional charge.

Crossfit Group Prep Course- $199

Crossfit Private Prep Course- $399

The Crossfit Private Prep Course consists of you and a coach working together one on one until you are sufficient at the 9 fundamental movements of Crossfit, this typically takes 8 one hour sessions. Generally people choose this option who can not make the times listed above for our 'Group' classes.
